3.A.9\\
$\varphi(z) = \bar{z}$, such that
\begin{equation}
	\varphi(w+z) = \bar{w+z} = \bar{w} + \bar{z} = \varphi(w) + \varphi(z)
\end{equation}
but
\begin{equation}
	\varphi(\lambda z) = \bar{\lambda z} = \bar{\lambda} \bar{z} \neq \lambda \bar{z} = \lambda \varphi(z)
\end{equation}
